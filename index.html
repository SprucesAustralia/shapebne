Scope of Work & Task Schedule

Task Details
Debris, Dust & Plastics RemovalSweep/vacuum large debris, dust all surfaces (walls, ceilings, fixtures), remove protective plastics/tapes, collect and bag construction waste.
Windows, Tracks & SealsClean interior glass surfaces, vacuum window tracks, wipe down frames and sills.
Walls & FixturesDust walls top-to-bottom, spot clean minor marks, wipe switch plates, outlets, light fixtures, vents, and joinery.
Kitchens & BathroomsDeep clean and sanitize sinks, countertops, toilets, showers/tubs; clean inside/outside of appliances; polish mirrors and fixtures.
Floors (General Clean)Sweep/vacuum all floor types, mop hard floors, clean baseboards, and perform a thorough vacuum of carpets.
Final Check & Fix-upsPerform final walkthrough against a detailed checklist to address any missed spots, smudges, or fingerprints, ensuring a dust-free environment.

General Exclusions

  • Exterior building cleaning
  • External window cleaning
  • Full carpet shampooing
  • Pressure washing concrete floors
  • High-access cleaning (above 2.5m)
