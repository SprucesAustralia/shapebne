Standard Discount Terms (FAFO)

Important: 5% Discount Condition

To achieve our FAFO price, a 5% discount is applied. This discount is conditional upon the following:

  • Uninterrupted Access: Our cleaning teams are granted access to complete areas after all construction work and trade activities in those areas have ceased.
  • No Re-cleans Due to Other Trades: The pricing assumes that areas will not require re-cleaning due to dust, debris, or traffic from other trades after our clean has been completed.

This allows our teams to work with maximum efficiency, and we pass those savings directly on to SHAPE.

Special Workflow Offer: Terms & Conditions

This proposal outlines a significant price reduction to $197,000 based on a workflow that provides our teams with uninterrupted access to completed areas. This structure is designed to eliminate the costs associated with re-cleaning, which are typically factored into standard quotes.

Proposed Workflow:

The discount is contingent upon the following four-stage process for each area:

  1. Construction/Build Phase: Your teams complete the primary building work.
  2. Initial Clean: Our team performs the builder's clean after your trades have vacated.
  3. Defects/Fixing Phase: Your teams re-enter to complete fixes or address defects.
  4. Final Clean: Our team performs the final, handover-ready clean.

Financial Impact & Core Conditions:

  • Discounted Price: By committing to this workflow, the total project price is reduced to $197,000.
  • Payment Terms: Invoices for each completed and billed stage must be paid in full no more than 38 days after the invoice date.

Policy for Interruptions During the Final Clean

We understand that despite careful planning, trades may need to re-enter a space during the project's final stages. This policy applies specifically to interruptions that necessitate re-cleaning during the Final Clean (stage 4) and does not affect the initial builder's clean.

Process for Additional Cleaning:

Should an interruption occur that requires us to re-clean an area (e.g., an electrician works on defects during the final carpet clean, leaving debris), the following process will be initiated:

  1. Report & Document: Our on-site team will immediately document the situation and the scope of the required re-cleaning.
  2. Submit for Approval: This documentation will be formally submitted to your site manager for approval before any extra work begins.
  3. Execute & Bill Separately: Once approved, the additional cleaning will be performed. These hours will be billed separately from the main contract price.

Billing for Approved Additional Hours:

To ensure fairness and transparency, any approved, additional hours for re-cleaning due to trade interruptions will be billed at a highly discounted, at-cost rate of $49 AUD per hour. This process ensures that you only pay for necessary, pre-approved work at a minimal rate, allowing you to maintain the significant overall project discount.
